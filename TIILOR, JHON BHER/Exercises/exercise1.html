McQueen

Lightning McQueen, the iconic protagonist of the "Cars" film series, is a sleek
and vibrant red race car adorned with the unmistakable number "95" proudly displayed
on his doors and roof. Voiced by Owen Wilson, McQueen starts as an arrogant and
self-centered character but undergoes a transformative journey throughout the trilogy.
