ABOUT BTS

        BTS, also known as 방탄소년단 (Bangtan Sonyeondan), is a musical ensemble consisting of seven members hailing from South Korea. 
        The group originated under the guidance of BigHit Music, now a part of HYBE Corporation, a South Korean
         entertainment entity founded by the song writer and producer Bang Shi Hyuk. In the face of initial challenges
          and resource constraints, BigHit embraced the philosophy of "music and artist for healing," creating a 
          nurturing space that empowered artists to cultivate their skills and progress autonomously.
