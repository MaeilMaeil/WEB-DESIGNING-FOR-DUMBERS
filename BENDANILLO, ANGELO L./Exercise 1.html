Introduction to Web Design

What is Web Design?

Web design encompasses the process of creating, planning, and arranging elements on a website to achieve both aesthetic appeal and optimal user experience. It involves considering factors such as layout, color scheme, typography, and functionality to effectively communicate the website's purpose and guide users through its content. Web designers employ various tools and techniques, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to bring their designs to life across different devices and browsers, ensuring seamless interaction and engagement for visitors.
