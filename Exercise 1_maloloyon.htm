TWICE Announces New Single ‘I Got You’: Here’s When It Arrives

Get ready for more new music, ONCEs! TWICE revealed the initial plans for its return in the forthcoming year. The K-pop group shared the a few sparse details for its upcoming single, as well as its release date.

“TWICE Pre-Release Single I GOT YOU Release on 2024.02.02 FRI 2PM KST/0AM EST,” the group shared via X on Thursday (Dec. 14).

In addition to the track’s release date, the K-pop stars revealed that the song is available for pre-order on iTunes and pre-save on Spotify. Fans in the United States will be able to purchase the single as a CD, which is available to be pre-ordered starting on Friday, Dec. 15. TWICE also teased the cover and CD art, with additional visual details for the physical release being revealed at a later date.

“I Got You” comes after TWICE’s most recent releases the Ready to Be EP in March and singles compilation album The Remixes in November. Ready to Be peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard 200, and spent a total of 11 weeks on the chart. The album’s lead single, “Moonlight Sunrise,” hit No. 84 on the all-genre Billboard Hot 100.

Also on the horizon for TWICE is a series of live shows starting in February. The K-pop stars will play four shows across Mexico and Brazil in 2024, followed by the “Ready to Be Once More” concert on March 16 at the Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas.
